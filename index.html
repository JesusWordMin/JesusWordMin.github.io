About Jesus Word Ministries

Sandra Bell graduated from Rhema Bible Training Center in 1980. She was ordained through the laying on of hands in 1981, administered by Wilfred Reidt, the son-in-law of Rev John G. Lake. She has been sent to Israel, Panama, Mexico, El Salvador, Ukraine, and sixteen other nations. She has spent the last twenty-two years in Asia, being used of the Lord mightily and seeing His glory manifest, as signs and wonders follow the Living Word she preaches.
